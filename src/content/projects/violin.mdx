At Austin Cunningham's 2025 Christmas show, he decided to try hiring two classically trained musicians to join his Country show: [Rachel Joy Ellis](https://www.instagram.com/joycellomusic/) on Cello and [Dr. Eugen Kim](https://www.instagram.com/eugenjazz/) on Violin. I didn't get a picture of the classical musicians, but, a memory from the concert:

<Image src={poorDavids} class="w-full m-b-6" alt="Austin Cunningham at Poor David's Pub" />

They accompanied Austin beautifully and a few moments from Dr. Kim struck me so hard that I started searching for used violins that night. After a bit of research, I learned that the safer route is renting and the next day on Christmas Eve I managed to pick one up from [Caraway Strings](https://www.carawaystrings.com/). I jumped to it and held a Twinkle Twinkle recital the next day for my family.

<Image src={christmas} class="w-full m-b-6" alt="Christmas violin recital of Twinkle Twinkle Little Star" />
